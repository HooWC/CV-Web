This project was dedicated to the design and development of a highly modern and responsive movie ticketing website designed to meet the needs of movie fans with a passion for film culture as well as the cinema industry. My goal was to create an impressive online platform by enhancing the user experience, optimizing accessibility, and enabling seamless navigation across a variety of devices.
