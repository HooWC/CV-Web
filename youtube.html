Youtube Music

Project information

  • Category: App design
  • Project date: 12 October, 2022
  • Language: Xamarin / Firebase
  • Project URL: No Hosting

The aim of this project is to design a mobile app that mimics YouTube Music, providing users with the ability to purchase music products, browse artist information, view the latest song list, and purchase music. My goal is to create an intuitive and easy-to-use music purchasing platform that allows users to easily discover and purchase their favorite music titles. I am committed to designing an intuitive, easy-to-use user interface that allows users to easily find the features they need and complete the purchase process. I will optimize the performance of the app to ensure fast loading speed and smooth operation experience to enhance user satisfaction.
