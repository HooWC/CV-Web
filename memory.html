This is my thesis project, developing a forum. The forum encompasses 88 topics and over 100 pages. Users can sign in, level up, earn titles, post threads, reply to others, as well as search for jobs and showcase personal works. The website offers a plethora of features aimed at assisting users in quickly finding solutions.
